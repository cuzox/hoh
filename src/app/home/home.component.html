Partnering to make God's love visible through Education, Housing & Health

What we do

Hands of Hope partners with communities, developing organizations, churches and leaders so that children's educational opportunities are increased, decent housing is developed and spiritual, physical and medical needs are addressed.

Activities

The Hands of Hope Child Sponsorship Program is comprehensive and holistic. It's dedicated to helping children find a path out of poverty through the love of Jesus Christ.

Our Projects

Hands of Hope staff work with the community and church leaders on an ongoing basis, providing consultation and assessment of progress, coordinating training for leaders and pastors, monitoring sponsored children and their families, etc.
