Child Sponsorship

The Hands of Hope Child Sponsorship Program is comprehensive and holistic. It's dedicated to helping children find a path out of poverty through the love of Jesus Christ. By working with local churches and community leaders, Hands of Hope offers educational opportunities, health care and health-related instruction, nutrition, life-skills training, and opportunities to hear about and respond to the Gospel.

Community Partnership

Hands of Hope has identified a number of communities, both urban and rural, for churches to partner with in developing long-range plans (generally about a 10 year project) to identify and prioritize community needs, such as schools, churches, medical clinics, community centers, water wells, and leadership mentoring. Plans are developed based on priorities, funding opportunities, and the short-term member skills and experience. Churches send short-term teams one or more times per year. Hands of Hope staff work with the community and church leaders on an ongoing basis, providing consultation and assessment of progress, coordinating training for leaders and pastors, monitoring sponsored children and their families, etc.

Short-term Mission Trips

Hands of Hope provides opportunities for churches to send teams to serve in Dominican Republic communities in a variety of ways, including building and remodeling houses, building latrines, leading Vacation Bible School, visiting sponsored children, sending medical professionals and supplies to provide basic health care assessments and information, and witness the love of Christ by building relationships.
